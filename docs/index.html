Mail Merge for Google Sheets

Send personalised emails at scale directly from Google Sheets — with open & click tracking, templates, bounce detection, and a built-in dashboard.

✉

Personalised Bulk Email

Merge any spreadsheet column into your email. Send hundreds of tailored messages in one go.

📈

Open & Click Tracking

Track who opened your email and which links they clicked — all logged back into your sheet.

📄

Reusable Templates

Save and manage email templates with merge fields. Switch between templates effortlessly.

📋

Live Dashboard

See delivery stats, open rates, and bounce counts at a glance from the sidebar.

🔒

Bounce Detection

Automatically flags bounced emails so you can keep your list clean and deliverability high.

⚙

Simple Setup

Install with clasp push, refresh your sheet, and you're ready. No external services required.

Quick Start

  1. Clone this repository and install clasp.
  2. Run clasp login and connect to your Google account.
  3. Create a Google Sheet, open its Apps Script editor, and copy the Script ID.
  4. Run clasp clone YOUR_SCRIPT_ID then clasp push.
  5. Deploy as a Web App for tracking support.
  6. Refresh your sheet — the Mail Merge menu appears automatically.
